Key Events

Tech Summit: Typically held in late September, the Tech Summit is a one-day conference that is designed to kickstart tech recruiting on campus. We have keynote speakers and panelists come in from top companies in the area, and we also have representatives from the Career Management Group at Haas explain key concepts, such as the difference between PM and PMM at various companies. There are also opportunities to listen to 2nd year students share their internship experiences.

Speaker Series: A multi-weekly event where alumni speakers come on campus once a week to talk about a breadth of topics in technology and to network with students.

Tech Challenge: 2-day event in which MBA teams from top business schools tackle a real-world problem from a sponsoring company. In the past two years, we've had sponsors from Ford Mobility and Google Fiber.

Career Treks: HTC organized trips to Google, Facebook, Airbnb, Amazon, and Microsoft, as well as many small-to-mid-sized companies throughout Fall and Spring.
