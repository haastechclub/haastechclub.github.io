Haas Technology Club

Connecting students with Silicon Valley

Previous Next

The MBA program at the Haas School of Business, University of California-Berkeley, is the premiere MBA program for those interested in the intersection of business and technology. Our highly distinguished faculty, accomplished alumni, and close proximity to Silicon Valley help prepare students from a variety of backgrounds for a career in the Tech Industry.

The Haas Tech Club (HTC) is one of the largest clubs at Haas with over 600 active students. We provide a forum for students to explore career opportunities within the Tech industry, from large companies to start-ups, foster links between students and members of the business community, and educate students about new technologies and trends.
